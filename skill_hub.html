Skill Hub: Shaping Future-Ready Educators

The Skill Hub by Adiuvaret Foundation is a comprehensive platform designed to empower educators with expertise in cognitive skills, preschool education, and skill evaluation. Our tailored programs focus on enhancing teaching methodologies, fostering critical thinking, and equipping educators with effective assessment techniques.

Cognitive Skill

Management for Pre-School Children

Cognitive skill is one of the most important skill that defines child's proficiency in reasoning, logical thinking and problem solving. Keeping the Montessori and Froebel methods in mind, very systematic, methodical and structured activities are needed to develop these skills in kindergarten children. Adiuvaret kids, through its in-depth research has designed 7 specific games, activities and methods that will help the cognitive skill development in a very systematic way.
