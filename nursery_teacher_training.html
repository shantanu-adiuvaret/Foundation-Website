Laying the Foundation for Lifelong Learning

Adiuvaret Foundation’s Nursery Teacher’s Training Program is designed to prepare educators for the crucial role of shaping young learners during their formative years. This program emphasizes child psychology, early childhood pedagogy, and interactive teaching methods to create engaging and nurturing learning environments.

The programme

Available in 3 time periods with the following features

This program offers flexible durations: the 1-year program with 12 modules and 175 hours of teaching, the 6-month program with 9 modules and 150 hours, and the 3-month program with 9 modules and 75 hours.
